## Overview

Capture One Pro Advanced is an industry-leading RAW photo editing application trusted by commercial photographers, fashion studios, and photo professionals worldwide for its exceptional color science, tethered shooting capabilities, and non-destructive editing workflow.

The color grading system gives you unmatched control over how RAW data is interpreted and rendered. Color balance, color editor, and skin tone tools work together to produce accurate, natural color that reflects the photographer's vision.

Tethered shooting connects directly to supported cameras, displaying captures on screen the moment the shutter fires. The live preview and instant review workflow is the standard in commercial and studio photography.

Layered masking with AI-powered subject selection, luminance range, and color range tools makes local adjustments precise and non-destructive, letting you target exactly the region that needs treatment without affecting the rest of the image.

For professional photographers who demand the highest quality RAW processing and the most capable studio workflow tool available, Capture One Pro Advanced is the editing platform that defines the professional standard.

## Performance

Capture One Pro balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
